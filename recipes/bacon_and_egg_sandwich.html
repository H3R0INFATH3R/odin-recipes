Bacon and Egg Sandwich

A sandwich made up of bacon and eggs

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • 3 slices crisp bacon
  • 1 fried egg
  • sliced tomatoes
  • Miracle Whip

Steps

  1. Spread 1 slice of bread with Miracle Whip.
  2. Add the fried egg.
  3. (While the egg is cooking, flatten it with a metal spatula to break the yolk-it fits the bread better.) Spread the egg thinly with Miracle Whip.
  4. Add crisp bacon in a single layer.
  5. Next, a layer of tomato slices.
  6. Cover with other slice of bread spread with Miracle Whip.
